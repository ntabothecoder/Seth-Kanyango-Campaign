Biography

Seth Kanyango's journey began in the heart of rural Kenya where education was scarce but dreams were big. Over 20+ years, Seth rose from a dedicated teacher to a national advocate for education reform. His leadership in local and regional unions set a benchmark for transparent, inclusive governance.

He believes in empowering teachers, investing in student success, and building a fair system for all. This campaign is a continuation of a lifelong mission to serve the educators who shape our nation.
